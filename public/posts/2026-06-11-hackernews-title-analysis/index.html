Hacker News Title Analysis

Intro

Hacker News is a forum popular within the startup community where people share their opinions and products. I like the venue because it frequently surfaces fun and niche insights about the trends within the community. I encountered this dataset on kaggle and thought it’d be interesting to analyze the titles. Unfortunately, this dataset doesn’t countain anything about the real post.
