Since I was in middle school I've been traveling. I've been to more than twenty cities in France, Germany, Croatia, Cezh Republic, Swiss, Spain, Italy and Japan before the age of 18. Because of my extensive experiences, I don't get a lot of people's travel anxiety and I don't get sick in transportations. In fact, transportations are a source of my enjoyment. I didn't get to enjoy it until I later grew up. My mom always travels with me before that and she put a lot of pressure on the otherwise supposed to be fun trip.
